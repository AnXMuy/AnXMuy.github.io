# 🧭 Research Statement

Building multimodal systems for fine-grained remote sensing understanding and interactive speech.

<div class="zh-note">
面向细粒度遥感理解与交互式语音系统的多模态研究。
</div>

My research focuses on remote sensing vision-language understanding, object-level image description, and interactive speech systems. I am particularly interested in building datasets, benchmarks, and models that enable fine-grained perception, reasoning, and language generation in real-world scenarios.

<div class="zh-note">
我的研究聚焦于遥感视觉语言理解、目标级图像描述与交互式语音系统，重点关注支持细粒度感知与推理的数据集、模型与评测基准。
</div>
